Daniel Guest

Postdoc working in Laurel Carney's lab at the University of Rochester. My current research focuses on building computational models of the auditory periphery and subcortex and applying them to better understand human auditory behavior, such as detection of complex sounds in noise. My interests intersect auditory psychophysics, physiology, and computational modeling. I am an advocate for high-quality and open scientific code; I program in MATLAB, Python, Julia, and C. Full CV
